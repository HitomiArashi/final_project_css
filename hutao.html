Hu Tao

Basic Information

Full Name: Hu Tao

Vision: Pyro

Nation: Liyue

Voice Actors: Rie Takahashi (JP), Tao Dian (CN)

Hu Tao is the 77th Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor, cheerful yet mysterious.

Abilities

NA: Polearm strikes.

Skill: Paramita Papilio – boosts attack at the cost of HP.

Ultimate: Spirit Soother – AoE Pyro DMG + HP recovery.

Talents: Increases Pyro DMG when HP is low.

Constellation

  • C1: Removes stamina consumption for charged attacks.
  • C2: Improves Blood Blossom damage.
  • C4: Enhances team buffs when HP is below 25%.
  • C6: Prevents lethal damage once every minute.

Summary

Hu Tao is a powerful Pyro DPS with high risk and high reward gameplay.
